Human Rights Advocacy

Promote Social Justice and Fundamental Human Right across the Country

The CCF envisions a society where justice and peace are seen as pivotal needs of all human beings and where access to justice is an inalienable right of all Nigerians irrespective of their social status.

We work to promote social justice, fundamental human rights, rule of law, accountability and good governance as critical elements of a civilised society through advocacy as it especially relates to the plights of the IDPs in the camps and in diaspora.
